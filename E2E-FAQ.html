FAQ

Curious about what happens behind the survey? From how your feedback is collected to how it turns into real action, this section gives you a clear view of the Engage to Elevate process, the dashboard, and how results will be shared across the organization.

1. What is the Engage to Elevate dashboard?

The dashboard is a dynamic platform powered by Qualtrics that presents the survey results in a clear and interactive way. It allows users to explore key insights, track trends, and better understand the collaborator experience across different areas.

2. How does the dashboard work?

Once the survey responses are collected, the data is processed, cleaned, and analyzed. The dashboard then visualizes this data through charts and insights, making it easier to identify strengths, gaps, and opportunities for improvement.

3. Who will have access to the dashboard?

Access to the dashboard will be provided in phases:

  • Operations department and leadership will have early access during May
  • The remainer of the population will receive access according the roadmap
4. When will the survey be conducted?

The Engage to Elevate survey will be conducted every two years. This timeline ensures there is enough time to:

  • Implement action plans
  • Monitor progress
  • Measure real impact before the next survey cycle
5. Are the survey responses confidential?

Yes. All responses are fully confidential.

The survey is managed through Qualtrics, a trusted third-party platform, which ensures that individual answers cannot be traced back to collaborators. This allows everyone to share honest and open feedback safely.

6. What happens after the survey results are available?

After the survey:

  1. Data is cleaned and analyzed in depth
  2. Key insights are identified
  3. Action plans are developed at different levels
  4. Progress is monitored over time

The goal is to turn feedback into concrete improvements.

7. Will employees see the results?

Yes. Once the data has been carefully analyzed and validated, a global view of the results will be shared with all collaborators to ensure transparency and alignment.

8. Why is there a delay before sharing results with everyone?

Before sharing results widely, the data goes through a deep cleaning and analysis process. This ensures:

  • Accuracy of insights
  • Protection of confidentiality
  • Meaningful and actionable outcomes
